The 2nd generation estate seller that's still second to none

Whether you’re a buyer or seller, the estate sale industry in Los Angeles can be particularly difficult to navigate. That’s why so many trust Hughes. Unlike competing companies and individuals which mostly "popped up" during the recession, we’re family owned, operated and have been the leading authority in estates sales for over 35 years. Our firm prides itself on providing a complete range of estate services across Southern California—and occasionally other parts of the country—all from the foothills of the San Gabriel Mountains, which we still call home today.

A Little Hughes History

Credited with helping ignite the re-birth of Old Town Pasadena’s antique and art scene, our founder Larry Hughes opened his first storefront named “Riff Raff” on Holly Street in 1978. Specializing in vintage California Pottery and 20th century art and collectibles, Larry then pioneered the antique mall concept when he opened Holly Street Bazaar in 1980. In its heyday, it housed 50 dealers and was regularly featured in travel magazines and collector’s guides from as far away as Japan. Larry then opened his first eponymous store in Pasadena’s Playhouse District, where he built a loyal following of estate sale customers who appreciated his eclectic mix of American, European and Asian antiques, collectibles and fine art. Larry retired in 2006 and handed over Hughes to his son, Todd.
